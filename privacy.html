Privacy Policy

Ghost Acquisitions LLC ("we," "us," "Ghost Acquisitions").

1. Who this applies to

This policy covers individuals and families who interact with Ghost Acquisitions — a life insurance marketing service — including those who request information or quotes, ask to speak with a licensed insurance professional, or opt in to receive messages from us.

2. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, mobile phone number, state of residence, and general details about the coverage you're seeking (such as household size or desired start date) that you submit through our forms or in conversation with us.

3. How we use your information

  • Respond to your request for life insurance information or quotes
  • Contact you via SMS about your inquiry, appointment scheduling and confirmations, and service updates
  • Connect you with a licensed insurance professional who can review options with you
  • With separate consent, send marketing messages about plan options, enrollment period reminders, and offers
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer support
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

4. SMS messaging & consent

We collect SMS consent through two separate, unchecked checkboxes on our opt-in form — one for transactional/customer-care messages and one for marketing messages. You may consent to either, both, or neither, and consent is never a condition of purchasing any product or service.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. To opt out at any time, reply STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP or contact us at support@ghostacquisitions.com.

We do not sell or share your mobile phone number or SMS consent data with any third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Your opt-in is used only to deliver the messages you selected from Ghost Acquisitions.

5. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only with (a) the licensed insurance professional handling your request, so they can assist you; (b) service providers who help us operate our platform and deliver messages (for example, our SMS provider and CRM), who are bound to use your information only to provide services to us; and (c) legal or regulatory authorities when required by law.

6. Data retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to assist with your request or as required by law. Opt-out requests are honored promptly and your number is suppressed from future outreach.

7. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting support@ghostacquisitions.com.

8. Cookies & analytics

Our website may use basic analytics to understand traffic and usage. We do not use cookies for advertising tracking.

9. Children's privacy

Our services are intended for adults seeking insurance coverage and are not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of our services after notice of changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
